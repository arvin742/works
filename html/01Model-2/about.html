About us

Guangdong SANDAR CNC Machinery Co., Ltd. was established in 1995, located in Beihai Industrial Area of Lunjiao, Shunde District, Foshan City, Guangdong Province, China. SANDAR has been awarded the certification of national high and new technology enterprise, passed ISO9001:2015 QMS certificate while products achieved CE and TUV certifications. SANDAR has obtained the certification of Adopting International Standard Product Marking, Guangdong High-tech Products and certification of Chinese GMC certified high quality manufacturer. As the 7th & 8th council president enterprise of Lunjiao Woodworking Machinery Chamber of Commerce, SANDAR is the quality brand that famous around China woodworking machinery industry.

SANDAR Machinery is positioning in domestic high-end market with the superior quality, advanced technology and stable performance. The core value of R&D is “Learning, Innovating, Surpassing”. Keep absorbing international advanced technology and experience continuously and innovate the design of equipments basic on the users’ actual production requirements. Therefore, the quality and technology of SANDAR machines have reached the highest levels domestically as well as international level standard. The advantage of SANDAR machines is the unique exclusive core technology of hardware and software with various patented designs, products with high technical contents, high efficiency, reliable quality and excellent appearance.

At present, SANDAR products mainly includes SKL series CNC labeling & processing center; SK series CNC panel saws; SW-400 series sliding table saws; SE 1,2 series edge banders; SKD series CNC drilling machines （5sides & 6 sides）and DW series boring machines, etc.
