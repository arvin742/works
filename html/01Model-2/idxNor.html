OEM/ODM

  • Initial consultation

    Careful consulting service with customers who need customized OME service in the early stage, through understanding customer needs, order quantity, delivery time

  • Customized delivery

    After the two sides reached a high degree of consensus, customers will send customized requirements to Baogao Hardware Enterprise mailbox by mail.

  • Deposit deposits

    After the communication and understanding of typesetting confirmation, the customer will give a certain amount of deposit to the designated account of Baogao Hardware. After the confirmation of the deposit amount, OME products enter the production cycle schedule.

  • Reach a consensus

    After early communication between customers and Baogao salesmen, customers highly recognized the quality and service of Baogao products and reached a high consensus between the two sides.

  • Typesetting confirmation

    A series of customized requirements, according to customer requirements for product prototype manufacturing, the cost of plate production, to be borne by customers.

  • Arrange shipments

    Customized products enter the production cycle, according to the agreed time, on time delivery.
