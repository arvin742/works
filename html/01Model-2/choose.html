Why Choose Us

Our company has earned the trust of our customers by performing transparent business activities and using client centric policies. We have become the trustworthy and prestigious company in the eyes of our clientele because of our quality assured product line and transparency in business deals.

At present, SANDAR products mainly includes SKL series CNC labeling & processing center; SK series CNC panel saws; SW-400 series sliding table saws; SE 1,2 series edge banders; SKD series CNC drilling machines （5sides & 6 sides）and DW series boring machines, etc.

To insist on the idea of precision manufacturing, remarkable quality and optimized service system, SANDAR products have reached the international level to the similar products globally. Products achieved the certification of “Adopting international Standard Product Marking”. With the characteristic of high precision, high speed and reliable quality, SANDAR machines are highly welcomed and praised by customers. SANDAR brand is firmly on the forefront of domestic woodworking machinery industry and built a high quality brand image. During the recent 2 decades, we provided over 10,000 sets equipments and production lines to thousands of furniture manufacturers. Domestic sales network has covered all major furniture production areas and exported all around the world. SANDAR has become a quality well-known enterprise in domestic woodworking machinery industry.

In the future, we will take the initiative to develop more varied of CNC machineries to respond to market demands, which can provide intelligent equipments for Intellectualized & customized furniture production. Sincerely, we are looking forward to establish friendly cooperation relationship with more and more customers from home and aboard. Constantly develop more international level products, which satisfying users’ requirements and step forward to become a global famous brand.
