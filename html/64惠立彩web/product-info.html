TS-1300 A

Digital Flatbed Inkjet Printer Model TS-1300 Aseries Features of Model TS-1300 A series

  • 1. Fast & Efficiency: 20 seconds to print a A4 size photo, can print up to 200 pcs per hour.
  • 2. Eco-friendly: The printed clothes are soft and comfortable and are harmless to the human body.
  • 3. Excellent effect: the printed photo is bright and colorful with good color fastness, the printed clothes are not fading and washable.
  • 4. Easy operation: No need manual decoding, just add ink directly to the8 boxes of 1.8L, easy and no worry.
  • 5. Wide application: Suitable for almost all kind of fabric such as cuttingpieces, sweater, ready-made garment, denim, feather dress, etc.
  • 6. Customized: Print garments and cutting pieces with one machine.From 3m to 15m, the machine length and platform can be customized
  • 7. Patent product: The structure of the garment flatbed obtained the nationalutility model patent, which solved the problem of garment and cutting printing with one machine
  • 8. Low maintenance cost: From the machine head to the body, every smalldetail is specially designed for digital direct injection. The maintenance costof accessory is low, and it reserves space for upgrading to the nextgeneration machine.
